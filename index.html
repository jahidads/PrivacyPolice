7. Frequently Asked Questions

How often is this privacy policy updated?

We review our privacy policy at least twice a year and update it as needed to reflect changes in our practices or relevant laws. Significant changes will be communicated through our website and email notifications to registered users.

Our commitment to Privacy by Design ensures we continuously improve our practices to eliminate deceptive patterns.

Do you collect data from children?

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Our services are not directed to children, and we have implemented age verification mechanisms to prevent underage data collection.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we take steps to remove that information promptly.

How do you handle international data transfers?

When transferring data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards as required by law, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by data protection authorities.

Our web design incorporates clear information about international data flows to ensure transparency for our global users.

Can I withdraw my consent after giving it?

Yes, you can withdraw your consent at any time through your account settings or by contacting our privacy team. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

We make consent management easy through our user-friendly interface, following best practices for website design in 2023.

How do you notify users of policy changes?

We notify users of material changes to our privacy policy through multiple channels:

  • Banner notification on our website for 30 days
  • Email notification to registered users
  • Updated "Last Modified" date prominently displayed
  • Version history available upon request

This multi-channel approach ensures users are properly informed of changes affecting their privacy rights.
